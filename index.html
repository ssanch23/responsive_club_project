Currently Reading

Project Hail Mary

Andy Weir

Ryland Grace is the sole survivor on a desperate, last-chance mission—and if he fails, humanity and the earth itself will perish. Except that right now, he can’t even remember his own name, let alone the nature of his assignment or how to complete it. Hurtling through space on this tiny ship, it’s up to him to puzzle out an impossible scientific mystery—and conquer an extinction-level threat to our species.

Genre: Science Fiction

The Memory Police

Yōko Ogawa

Objects are disappearing: first hats, then ribbons, birds, roses—until things become much more serious. Most are oblivious to these changes, while those with the power to recall the lost objects live in fear of the draconian Memory Police, who are committed to ensuring that what has disappeared remains forgotten. When a young woman discovers that her coworker is in danger from the Memory Police, she concocts a plan to hide him.

Genre: Science Fiction
